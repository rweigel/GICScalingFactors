GIC Scaling Factors

Interactive maps of data in "A novel approach for geoelectric field response scaling factors used in geomagnetic storm hazard assessments" by A. Pulkkinen, P. Schuck, E. Bernabeu, R. Weigel and R. Arritt.

Maps are derived using data data/waveforms_All_2025.mat in the repository for the maps.

The four maps are:

  • MEA
  • MMB
  • NUR
  • OTT
